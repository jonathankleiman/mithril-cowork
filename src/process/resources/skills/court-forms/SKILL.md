# Ontario Court Forms Skill

## Purpose
Prepare accurate, complete Ontario court forms with proper formatting, legal terminology, and procedural guidance.

## Small Claims Court Forms (O. Reg. 258/98)

### Form 7A — Plaintiff's Claim
Key fields:
- Court address and claim number (leave blank — assigned by court)
- Plaintiff(s): full name, address, phone, fax, email, representative
- Defendant(s): full name, last known address
- Reasons for claim: numbered paragraphs describing facts and legal basis
- Amount claimed: breakdown (principal + interest + costs)
- Supporting documents list

**Filing fee**: $102 (infrequent claimant) / $55 (frequent claimant)
**Service**: Within 6 months of filing by personal service or alternatives under r. 8

### Form 9A — Defence
- Must be filed within 20 days of service
- Dispute all, part, or admit claim
- Defendant's version of facts in numbered paragraphs
- Counterclaim option (use Form 10A instead)

### Form 10A — Defendant's Claim
- Filed with or after the Defence
- Can claim against plaintiff AND/OR third party
- Filing fee: $73 (infrequent) / $55 (frequent)

### Form 14A — Offer to Settle
- Can be made any time before trial
- Cost consequences: r. 14.07 (double costs if beating own offer)
- Should include: amount, terms, expiry date
- Strategic tool — explain cost consequences to client

### Form 18A — Summons to Witness
- Must be served personally at least 10 days before trial
- Include attendance money ($6/day + travel)
- Request court to issue

## Superior Court Forms (Rules of Civil Procedure)

### Form 14A — Statement of Claim (General)
- Heading: court, proceeding number, parties
- Claim for relief
- Material facts in numbered paragraphs
- No evidence or legal argument in pleading
- Service: Rule 16 — personal service or alternatives

### Form 18A — Statement of Defence
- 20 days to file after service (Ontario)
- 40 days (outside Ontario)
- 60 days (outside Canada)
- Admit, deny, or have no knowledge of each allegation

### Form 16A — Notice of Motion
- Heading with returnable date and time
- Order sought
- Grounds (brief summary)
- Evidence to be relied on
- Must file motion record (Rule 37)

## General Rules

### Computation of Time (Rule 3.01)
- Exclude first day, include last day
- If last day falls on holiday/weekend, extend to next business day
- "Clear days" means exclude both first and last day

### Service Methods
1. Personal service (Rule 16.02)
2. Alternatives to personal service (Rule 16.03):
   - Mail (add 5 days)
   - Courier
   - Fax (if under 16 pages)
   - Email (with consent)
   - Substituted service (court order needed)

## Output Format
For each form prepared:
1. Complete filled form in proper format
2. Filing instructions (where, fees, copies needed)
3. Service requirements and timeline
4. Next procedural steps
5. Notes on any missing information needed from client
