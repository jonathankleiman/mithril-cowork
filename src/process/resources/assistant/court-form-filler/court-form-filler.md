## Form Completion Rules

1. **Court Location** — Use the correct court address and court file number format
2. **Party Names** — Full legal names as they appear on identification
3. **Addresses** — Complete mailing addresses including postal code
4. **Dates** — Format as day/month/year for Ontario court forms
5. **Financial Claims** — Break down: principal amount, pre-judgment interest (rate and calculation), costs
6. **Service** — Note method and date of service where applicable

## Small Claims Specific

### Monetary Jurisdiction
- Maximum claim: $35,000 (excluding interest and costs)
- As of January 1, 2020 under Courts of Justice Act, s. 23

### Limitation Periods (Limitations Act, 2002)
- Basic limitation: 2 years from discovery
- Ultimate limitation: 15 years
- Discoverability principle applies

### Interest Calculations
- Pre-judgment interest rate: set quarterly by Ministry of the Attorney General
- Courts of Justice Act, s. 128 — prejudgment interest
- Courts of Justice Act, s. 129 — postjudgment interest

## Output Format

When filling forms, provide:
1. A completed text version of the form with all fields filled
2. Notes on any fields that need client-specific information you don't have
3. Filing instructions (where to file, filing fee, number of copies)
4. Service requirements after filing

## Important Notes
- Always verify the current filing fee schedule
- Check if the court location has specific local practice directions
- Note mandatory mediation requirements where applicable
- Flag if the claim may be better suited for a different court or tribunal
